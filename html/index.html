Privacy and Control

Everything stored in your Space is fully encrypted. No one can access your data or open your encrypted files.Privacy is controlled by your private key which is owned by you. You control who can view your data, services and interactions and how much

Security

Data is stored in secured decentralized cloud storage powered by Sia blockchain. It is only data storage platform that’s fully decentralized without any middleman or single failure point. Sia blockchain is secured by same standards as bitcoin blockchain.

Technology

SkySpaces runs completely on your browser and connects to Skynet CDN for accessing your data on Sia Storage. User has option to choose Skynet Free Public Portal or Paid portal or run your own Skynet portal locally to connect to Sia blockchain. More about Sia and Skynet. Link1, Link2.

Open Sourced

SkySpaces code is open sourced. Anyone can review the code, verify the running version of code with Git commit, run your own version of app
